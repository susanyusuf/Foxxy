Project concept

Maintainer Compass

A lightweight dashboard for maintainers to plan releases, organize onboarding, and recognize contributors.

  • Contributor onboarding checklists
  • Release planning templates
  • Community health insights

Why this matters

Open-source projects grow faster when maintainers have structure.

Many promising projects stall because maintainers are doing everything by hand: documentation, onboarding, issue triage, roadmap planning, and contributor recognition. This project helps turn that chaos into a calm, repeatable workflow.

What I will build

A maintainers’ workspace with templates, checklists, milestone views, and simple analytics to reduce admin overload.

Who it helps

New maintainers, growing open-source teams, and contributors who want clearer guidance and better collaboration.

Why I’m the right maintainer

I’m focused on making tools that are beginner-friendly, practical, and built with real community needs in mind.

Roadmap

From early prototype to community-ready release.

Phase 1

Prototype the onboarding workflow and release dashboard.

Phase 2

Gather feedback from contributors and maintainers, then refine the UX.

Phase 3

Launch the public version with documentation and contributor guides.
